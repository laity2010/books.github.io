Growth Rates No other ingredient in discounted cash flow valuation evokes as much angst as estimating future growth. Unlike cash flows and discount rates, where we often have the security of historical data, growth rates require us to grapple with the future. This section looks at why growth rates can be different for equity and operating earnings. It examines two of the standard approaches for estimating growth (looking at the past, and using analyst estimates). This section closes with a discussion of the fundamentals that determine growth.
